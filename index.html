The 5th Washington High School annual 5k running event will be held on 3/25/2017. This event is to raise money for school’s special Ed program. This year’s goal is $10,000.

The event will start at 9:00am on the day. The route starts at Washing High School football court, going down on Main street , Walnut Street, Jefferson Ave and finish at the Rivercrest Park at Jefferson Ave.
