5K running 2017

March 25, 2017

The Washington High School annual 5k running event will be held on 3/25/2017. The event will start at 9:00am on the day. The route starts at Washing High School football court, going down on Main street , Walnut Street, Jefferson Ave and finish at the Rivercrest Park at Jefferson Ave. Awards, prizes and refreshments after the race.
